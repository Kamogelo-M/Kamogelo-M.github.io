Featured Poetry

Poem Title : Insecurity 

Poet : Karabelo

I have closed myself in this room for as long as i can remember...

Right now there is light on the other side of the door...

It scares me as it wants to come in...

I want to open the door but my mind & my body are numb...

I can't move anything to reach to the door...

My heart tells me that the only way to open this door...

I have to have patience, serinity, self-love, courage and most of all inner-peace...

To learn to let go what's in the past that broke me and be proud of myself...

Also LOVE what i have now...

The question in my head was, how and where to begin this process...

The answer from my heart was communication.

.Kay_Wonder
